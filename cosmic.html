Cosmic Force - Part I

by Lazar Pantic

Somewhere in-universe, the most powerful weapon in the entire multiverse is broken. At that moment planets around explode, rifts open, the entire multiverse sense disturbance. Universes begin to collapse. Infinity team ship arrived, heroes jumped out of the ship and they tried to help people.

Infinity team is a group of superheroes who travel in the multiverse. Team members are Thunderman (he is a leader, has the power to control weather and ice), Akia (she is a magic user), Dominik (has bulletproof skin), Stretch (can elongate his body), Cactus (the half human-half plant creature, has ability to shapeshift into plants and throw cactus spikes) and Dreks (he is ninja).

Cactus: What is happening?

Thunderman: No, no, no!

Akia: What is it?

Thunderman: I saw those rifts before! No, I will make sure that that weapon is in a dead universe.

Dreks: Pull yourself together! What the hell are you talking about?

Thunderman: Dimensional spear, the most powerful weapon. Someone has it!

Dominik: There are people in that building!

Dreks, Dominik and Cactus start running towards the building, but from one portal dragon Takija came.

Takija: I was looking for you! Your ship is something that can ruin my plans.

Stretch: Dragon! We never fought dragon before! This is new!

Takija breaks ship destroying its engine, Draks attacked the dragon, but dragon in one punch fainted Dreks, same with cactus and Dominik.

Thunderman: Wait! We cannot stop him like this! We need a plan.

Stretch: Here is a plan, you and Akia run, jump in one of those rifts and find help. I will slow him.

Thunderman: I am not leaving you guys!

Akia with her magic powers pushed Thunderman in one rift, he falls down on the ground, his costume was torn. Thunderman lost his consciousness, but someone came to him (just legs are shown and they belong to Professor).

In another dimension, in one room there are Quad and Ricky, they are saving aliens. Ricky, 17 year’s old guy, human, he is part of Space Patrol. Quad, green alien, is a leader of the Space Patrol.

Ricky: Oh no!

Quad: What is it?

Ricky started running.

Quad: Sh*t!

Their two friends were on ground, Ghost and K9.

Ricky: They are still breathing!

Overkill is human with hood and gas mask.

Overkill: Damn, what happened here?

Quad: We don’t know. Ricky, take the ship! Bring them to a safe place!

Ricky: There is no safe place!

Overkill: God dammit, kid! Just go!

Ricky took the ship and fly away. A mysterious person came out of the rift. The Professor is older man, with scientist coat.

Professor: Good, you two are still alive! I have one question for you two. Do you want to save multiverse?

Overkill and quad look each other.

Overkill: Hell yeah!

In Silverfang dimension

Silverfang is a knight in metal armor. Silverfang is fighting other knight, the earth around them is collapsing. He manage to defeat enemy.

Professor: Very impressive! And now you need to save a multiverse!

In Lizard’s dimension. Lizard (or John) is a humanoid lizard, superhero. He is running down town helping people. He is talking to Elizabeth (his girlfriend) by computer communicator in his ear.

Lizard: Elizabeth?

Elizabeth: I am here.

Lizard: What happened?

Elizabeth: To be honest, I thought it was something connected to you.

Lizard: What? What could be connected to me in this situation?

Elizabeth: I don’t know, it is always something with you.

Professor jumped out of rift.

Professor: What a wonderful coincidence! I was looking for you John.

Lizard: How do you know my name?

Professor: I know many things, but now we have multiverse to save!

Lizard: I am not going!

Elizabeth: Idiot! Go! I will call others for help.

Professor: That is brilliant idea dear! Someone has to keep this people save, I can’t bring everyone on this dangerous mission.

Elizabeth: He can hear me?

Lizard: It looks like. Sorry love, I have to go now.

Professor went to universe, but saw space station in flame.

Professor: Oh, no! I am too late! Well, what can you do? I cannot save all of them now!

In one room, there are Lizard, Quad, Overkill, Silverfang and Professor. Thunderman is lying on the bed.

Professor: You are all wondering why you are here?

Lizard: No man, you told as that we need to save multiverse, that’s why we are here.

Professor: Indeed.

Quad: We know why we are here, but why us?

Professor: I’m afraid that collapse came early in other dimensions, you are what I can find.

Overkill: So, we are the last option?

Lizard: But, why us? They are other people in our dimensions!

Professor: Quad and Overkill stopped the second war, Silverfang defeated a lot of knights and monsters, Lizard saved so many people and bring them home and this fellow on couch, he neglected his life to help others.

Silverfang: And he is dead now!

Professor: He is not dead!

Lizard: He looks dead to me.

Quad: Hey Overkill! He has a mask, he is your colleague.

Professor: Silence! We don’t have time for joking!

Thunderman: Where am I? Noooo! Akia!

Professor: Calm down boy! If you want to save your girlfriend, you will have to restore peace to the multiverse first.

Thunderman: She is not my girlfriend!

Quad: Oh great! We are in team with teen! I thought, Ricky is hard to work with.

Professor: You should not talk like that with team leader!

Thunderman: Wait, I am team leader?

Professor: Of course you are! You started all of this!

Thunderman: I have my own team, I am not…

Professor: Don’t make this thing harder, you are leader!

Overkill: Hey! Chill! No one will listen to you anyway.

Quad: You're scared of the competition, Overkill?

Thunderman: What we can do?

Silverfang: The kingdoms were destroyed. I watched people die! The magic is dead! There is nothing that can be done!

Professor: Not in every Earth! I will tell you a story. Back in the days, there was only one dimension, Earth Prime. I don’t why it has that name when it is entire universe. Anyways, Takija, name of dragon that you fought, was an immortal being who served and helped people. The world was perfect, without violence, deception and evil, but Takija was bored. He wanted to create new worlds to protect. He managed to do it, he created a multiverse, but, it had a price. His dimension was the source of energy for creating a multiverse. No one knows how many dimensions he created. When he returned back, his dimension died and portal was closed. For centuries, he lived alone in his dimension, knowing that he killed his world.

Silverfang: How did he find the way to leave Earth prime and to do this?

Thunderman: There were seven powerful weapons. One of them was dimensional spear. To stop our enemies to use it, me and my friends open a portal and send weapon to that Earth, thinking that no one is there no one that can use it.

Lizard: You were wrong!

Professor: Well, it’s not his fault. The spear did not go there.

Thunderman: What?!

Professor: I mean… Yes, but only one part! Takija had to make rest of it, which is why spear was unstable.

Quad: I know what you're trying to say. We need to find the rest of the spear!

Professor: Exactly!

The communicator on the table was activated.

- END OF PART ONE -
