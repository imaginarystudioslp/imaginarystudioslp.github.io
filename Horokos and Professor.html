Horokos and Professor

written by Lazar P. (Comic Tyrant) and George Ive

drawn by Adel and DeMon-49

Comic book series

This story follows two characters: Horokos, a very skilled user of dark magic, and Professor, an android with a high level of intelligence. They live together in a small town with many mysteries and explore paranormal activities.

You can visit the following links to support creators: Demon-49 on DeviantArt, Demon-61 on Twitter, Adel on Instagram, ComicTyrant on DeviantArt.
