William

Design, colors and character by Lazar P.

Design sheet by Demon-49

Description:

The leader of group called The Supremes.

Tall, a bit muscular, in his 40s, shaved, and well-groomed. Has black hair and a modern hairstyle, undercut.

He thinks that he is always right. He is also a great seducer - he can have any girl that he wants. He constantly changes them and can't have a stable relationship because of his behavior.

He doesn't accept the fact that he is getting old.

Wears a spacesuit (which is also armor), a rocket backpack and rocket boots.

Has a circle on his chest, that can release a large amount of energy.
