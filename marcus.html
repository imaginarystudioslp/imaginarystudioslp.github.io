Marcus (Silver Tiger)

Design, colors and character by Lazar P.

Design sheet by Demon-49

Description:

Marcus is Gertude's ex-husband, he spent 8 years in isolation where he had no contact with other people, to solve his anger problems, but it didn't help him much.

He always has an angry expression on his face.

Medium height, shorter than Gertude, but big and muscular, bald with a big black beard, in his late 30s.

He can turn into a humanoid tiger, hence the name Silver Tiger. The Tiger has large teeth and large claws.

He wears wide black pants, a dark gray sleeveless shirt, and a long black sleeveless coat. He is barefoot.
