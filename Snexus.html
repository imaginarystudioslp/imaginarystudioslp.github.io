Story:

SupremeNexus has a special ability from birth, He can manipulate energy and turn it into his own. He hid this ability from everyone. He was a citizen of Nexus City near Utopia. Nexus City got its name from the beacon that was in the center of the city and was a symbol of hope. With the Wicks Invasion, the beacon fell and people began to lose hope. SupremeNexus decided to step up, he revealed his ability by manipulating the energy of The Wicks and preventing them from exploding. When the other residents saw this, hope began to return to them. SupremeNexus picked up the ripped street lamp and used it as a weapon, over which he was releasing his new energy, becoming a new symbol of hope, hence the name SupremeNexus. He doesn't want to get into conflicts that don't concern him, he likes to make strategies and plans of attacks, and he doesn't like irresponsibility.
