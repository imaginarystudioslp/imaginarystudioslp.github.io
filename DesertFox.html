Story:

Desert Fox got that name, ever since the arrival of The Wicks, he roamed the desert and set traps to capture The Wicks, which he later interrogated and experimented on. The townspeople of the desert village claim to have seen him playing dead and The Wicks shunned him for it, all claiming he was as cunning as a fox, hence the name Desert Fox. Because they had the same goal, he readily agreed to join MUB. It took him some time to adjust, but it turned out that he works much better in the group. As for his personality, he spends a lot of time alone, working on his inventions. He's an excellent innovator, he's quite shy toward strangers. He doesn't like to get into unnecessary arguments. Also, he's not the best direct fighter.
