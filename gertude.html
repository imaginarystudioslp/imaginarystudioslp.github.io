Gertude

Design, colors and character by Lazar P.

Design sheet by Demon-49

Description:

She is a witch. She has big black pupils in her eyes, and she is in her early thirties, but is very attractive.

Her face and body shape are perfect, that's why guys fall for her.

She is the mother of the group, the former wife of the Silver Tiger.

There are three green circles on each hand, which represent a "mana" for her magic.

She has long light gray hair and a red dress, which is torn below knees.
