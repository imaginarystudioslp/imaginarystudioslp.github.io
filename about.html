About



Imaginary Studios represents a group of creative people who like to draw comic books, create new characters and to write interesting stories. The team was created after Lazar's desire to see his stories drawn into comics

The original team members are: L.P (ComicTyrant), Nemanja. N, Alex (Desert-Stalker), SpaceLizz and Adel xy.
