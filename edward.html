Edward

Design, colors and character by Lazar P.

Design sheet by Demon-49

Description:

He is a 22-years-old scientist and captain of the so-called Intergalactic Unit.

He has a very small beard as if he hadn't shaved for a couple of days, and dark circles under his eyes.

He is addicted to morphine.

He is a type of person that would do anything to protect his "family", even when he is angry at them.

Tall and thin, not muscular. Has black hair.

He always carries a checkered box with him. The box is the size of his fist, and when thrown to the ground it can open a portal.
