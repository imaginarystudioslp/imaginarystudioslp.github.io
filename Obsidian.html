Story:

Obsidian was a warrior before The Wicks attacked. He was first in line to defend his city. After a long battle that lasted all night, the city was saved and The Wicks retreated. Obsidian was the only one who came back alive but was seriously injured. He was admitted to the hospital immediately. His right hand was amputated. In addition to that, his left eye was badly injured. Obsidian recovered physically, but not mentally. No one saw him as a fighter anymore but as a wounded man. Despite everything that happened to him, Obsidian found ways to help others. On one mission, he helped the MUB Agents, and Desert Fox made him a mechanical arm out of respect, so that Obsidian could once again defend his city. He is a motivated and dedicated man with a forgiving nature and a positive attitude.
