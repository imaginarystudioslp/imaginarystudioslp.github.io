Kim

Created by ComicTyrant, drawn by V13

Properties:

• Real name: Kim

• Type: Elf

• Gender: Female

• Allegiance: MUB

Story:

Kim is an elf from the land of winds named "Kreidens". Kreidens had a special thing about it, it was not only a land of winds and elves but also a land where peace reigned. You could say that Kreidens was a haven for various creatures until The Wicks appeared. Their terror reached the borders of Kreidens. Kim took the task of stopping them. She went to the forest, near Wicks base, where Kaze found himself. Seeing how he fights and how he tries to protect the others, a romance arose between Kaze and Kim happened. She decided to join his organization and together with him, expel Wicks from their planet. As for her personality, she is too worried and devoted to keeping the peace. She is strong but clumsy, sees only the good in people, ignores negative things, and even tho she likes Kaze, she enjoys teasing him from time to time. She is an adult, but you could say she's childish.
