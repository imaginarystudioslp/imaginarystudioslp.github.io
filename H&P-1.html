Horokos and Professor

- #1 Under the full moon -

written by Lazar P. (Comic Tyrant) and George Ive, drawn by De-Mon61 and Adel (cover page)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Horokos and Professor in their first adventure together.

This story follows two characters: Horokos, a very skilled user of dark magic, and Professor, an android with a high level of intelligence.

They live together in a small town with many mysteries and explore paranormal activities.
