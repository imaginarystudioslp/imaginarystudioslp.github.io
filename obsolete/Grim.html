Story:

Grim, is a literal angel of death, a shinigami, who sensed the chaos the Wicks were wreaking. He renounced his immortality and descended among the living in order to restore the balance between the living and the dead. Since no one knows who he really is, he made a secret deal with Kaze and the MUB to serve as their secret agent, to gather first-hand information on the Wicks, so that when the time comes, they have everything they need to defeat them. As for his personality, he tries to be serious, but he can't help himself and his sarcastic side prevails. It's hard for him to get used to the fact that he is now mortal and that he has renounced the divine power. He takes his task seriously and is ready to go all the way there.
