Story:

Horokos is a scholar who went too far with learning black magic. Amongst other activities, he is also working as an investigator of paranormal activities. He is living in a small town with his good friend Professor. They both formed the PAA (Paranormal Action Agency). They are working on solving numerous mysteries in that small town.
